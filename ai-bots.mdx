# 🤖 MagicBots - External AI Chatbots

**MagicBots** are custom-trained chatbots that can be integrated into external sites and serve as customer support agents, providing users with an automated and personalized experience.

---

## 🔄 Manage Your MagicBots

To get started with managing your MagicBots, head to the **AI Bots** section:

- **Back to Dashboard**: Return to your dashboard at any time.
- **MagicBots**: View and manage your active external chatbots.
- **Add New Chatbot**: Create a new chatbot for your website.

---

## ➕ Adding a New Chatbot

To add a new chatbot, follow these steps:

1. Click **"Add New Chatbot"** to create and configure your AI chatbot.
2. The chatbot creation process includes **4 main steps**:
   - **Step 1**: Configure the chatbot to ensure it delivers accurate information.
   - **Step 2**: Customize the look and feel of your chatbot.
   - **Step 3**: Train the chatbot to personalize its responses.
   - **Step 4**: Embed the chatbot into your website using the generated code.

---

## 🔧 Step 1: Configure Your Chatbot

This step allows you to set up the essential functionality of your chatbot.

### Configuration Fields:

| Field                  | Description                                      |
|------------------------|--------------------------------------------------|
| **Chatbot Title**       | Give your chatbot a title (e.g., MagicBot).      |
| **Bubble Message**      | The initial message your chatbot will display (e.g., "Hey there, How can I help you?"). |
| **Welcome Message**     | The greeting message when users first interact with the chatbot (e.g., "Hi, how can I help you?"). |
| **Chatbot Instructions**| Provide clear instructions on what the chatbot can do (e.g., "Assist with customer support queries"). |
| **Do Not Go Beyond Instructions** | Set this option to prevent the chatbot from providing information outside of the defined instructions. |
| **Language**            | Select the chatbot's language (Auto detects or choose manually). |
| **AI Model**            | Choose the AI model |

> ⚙️ **Tip:** Make sure your instructions are clear to ensure the chatbot can respond accurately to user inquiries.

---

## 🎨 Step 2: Customize Your Chatbot

Customize your chatbot’s appearance to match your brand.

### Customization Options:

- **Upload Logo**: Upload a logo that represents your chatbot (optional).
- **Avatar**: Select an avatar image for your chatbot to personalize the interaction.
- **Color**: Choose an accent color that represents your brand's color palette.
- **Show Logo**: Option to display or hide your logo.
- **Show Date and Time**: Enable or disable date and time display in the chat.
- **Transparent Trigger**: Set the transparency of the chatbot trigger icon.
- **Trigger Avatar Size**: Adjust the avatar size (e.g., 60px).
- **Position**: Select whether the chatbot appears on the **left** or **right** side of the screen.

---

## 🧠 Step 3: Train Your Chatbot

This step is optional but highly recommended to personalize the chatbot experience.

### Training Methods:

You can train your chatbot using different content sources:
- **Website**: Provide URLs to allow the bot to learn from specific pages.
- **PDF**: Upload documents that the chatbot can use to improve responses.
- **Text**: Provide text-based training data.
- **Q&A**: Provide frequently asked questions and answers.

> 💡 **Tip:** Training the chatbot will improve its ability to answer questions accurately and engage users more effectively.

---

## 🔗 Step 4: Test and Embed

After configuring, customizing, and training your chatbot, it's time to embed it into your website.

### Embed Code:

Once the chatbot is ready, you will **get the embed code** that you can use to add the chatbot to your site.

```
<script defer src="https://app.robiai.com/vendor/chatbot/js/external-chatbot.js" data-chatbot-uuid="your-chatbot-id" data-iframe-width="chatbot-width" data-iframe-height="chatbot-height" ></script>
```


### Instructions:
1. Copy the embed code provided.
2. Paste it just before the closing `</body>` tag in your HTML file.
3. Save the changes and refresh your site to ensure the chatbot works properly.

## 🧑‍🤝‍🧑 Managing Active Chatbots

Once you’ve created and configured your chatbots, they will appear under the **Active Chatbots** section.

You can manage your active bots using the following options:

- **Avatar**: The bot's avatar will be displayed.
- **Title**: The name/title of your chatbot.
- **Creation Date**: The date when the chatbot was created.
- **Status**: See if the chatbot is active or passive.
- **Actions**: Click the three dots to reveal options for **Edit**, **Customize**, **Train**, **Test & Embed**, **Activate** (switch between active/passive), and **Delete**.
