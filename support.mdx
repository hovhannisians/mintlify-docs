# 🆘 Support Requests

If you need help with any issue related to Robi AI, you can easily create a support request. This allows us to assist you in resolving any issues quickly.

---

## 📝 Creating a New Support Request

To create a new support request:

1. Click on **"Create New Support Request"**.
2. Fill out the following fields:

- **Support Category**: Choose the appropriate category for your request (e.g., **General Inquiry**).
- **Support Priority**: Select the priority level (e.g., **Low**).
- **Subject**: Provide a brief summary of the issue or question.
- **Message**: Describe your issue in detail so we can assist you effectively.

3. After filling out the form, click **"Send"** to submit your request. Our team will get back to you as soon as possible.

---

## 📋 Support Ticket Management

Once your support request is submitted, you can manage and track it in the **Support Requests** section:

- **Ticket ID**: A unique identifier for your support request.
- **Status**: The current status of your request (e.g., Open, Closed).
- **Category**: The category you selected for your request.
- **Subject**: The brief summary of your request.
- **Priority**: The priority level you set for your request.
- **Created At**: The date and time when your request was created.
- **Last Updated**: The date and time when the support team last updated the ticket.
- **Actions**: Options to view or manage your ticket.
